# Plistic Landing Page Section Plan

This first build is a launch landing page for Plistic, not a clone of the old Podplistic site or the draft mockup.

## Homepage Sections

1. Launch offer banner - dismissible, editable copy for the early access launch offer.
2. Header navigation - anchors to the launch homepage sections: Services, Our Work, Pricing, Earn With Us, Blog, About, Contact.
3. Hero - animated prefix identity, tagline, CTAs, showreel-ready visual treatment, and "Made in Scotland" message.
4. Proof and trusted-by - only verified proof from the brief, plus client logo bar.
5. Services overview - four top-level service families with links to the future Services page.
6. Our Work preview - four launch case-study slots from the brief.
7. Made in Scotland / About - Scottish roots and psychology-informed production positioning.
8. Pricing path - quote estimator preview and booking/payment flow expectations.
9. Earn With Us - referral and partner programme teaser with referral CTA.
10. Blog/resources - launch categories and newsletter sign-up slot.
11. Closing CTA - book/quote conversion before the footer.
12. Footer - contact, location, launch nav, legal links.

## Explicit Exclusions

- No "Future Vision" software-product section. The brief says this is no longer accurate.
- No unverifiable stats from the draft mockup such as 50+ shows, 2M+ listens, or 3x growth.
- No visual cloning of the Podplistic Vercel mockup. It can inform content hierarchy only.

## Experiment Folders

Reviewable HTML variations live under `public/sections/<section-name>/experiments/` and are listed at `/experiments` when the dev server is running.
